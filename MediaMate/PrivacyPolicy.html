MediaMate Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

No Data Collection

MediaMate does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All media processing happens entirely on your device.

On-Device Processing

All video and audio conversions are performed locally on your iPhone or iPad. No files are uploaded to any server. No data leaves your device.

No Analytics or Tracking

MediaMate does not include any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising software. We have no way to know how you use the app.

Permissions

  • Photo Library — Only accessed when you choose a file for conversion or save a converted file. You control every selection.
  • Files & Documents — Only accessed when you pick a file from the Files app. Read-only access to your chosen file.

Data Storage

Converted files are saved to your device's local Documents directory. You can share, save to Photos, or delete them at any time. MediaMate does not sync or back up any files to cloud services.

Third-Party Services

MediaMate does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that process your data.

Children's Privacy

MediaMate is rated 4+ and does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date at the top will be updated. Your privacy rights will not be reduced without notice.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
